Welcome to Apache Probabilistic Assessment Tracking and Calibration (PATC) System

The PATC system is a platform to improve business decision by forecast training.

The PATC system aims to facilitate the industry in training super forecastor to assist the managmenet layer in making better business decisions.

The PATC system tries to compose a web based information management pipeline to support end to end data processing in an interactive fashion.

The PATC system involves the three primary functions (a) Uncertainty calibration training, (b) Probabilistic forecast tracking and calibration, and (c) Forecast correction.
